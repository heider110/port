About me

I am a driven and eager junior full stack developer with a passion for web development. I have more than three years of experience in coding and have acquired a solid understanding of front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as back-end technologies like Node.js and PHP.

I have also gained experience in database management and have worked with both SQL and NoSQL databases.

I am a fast learner and have a strong desire to constantly improve my skills. I am eager to take on new challenges and work with a team of experienced developers to further my development and growth.

I have completed several projects that demonstrate my ability to understand and apply complex coding concepts and deliver high-quality results. I am a team player and have excellent communication skills, making me a valuable member of any development team.

I am also able to work independently and am confident in my ability to learn quickly and efficiently. I am excited to bring my passion and potential to a full stack development role where I can contribute to the success of projects and continue to grow as a developer.
